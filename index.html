What We Offer

Our Services

We provide innovative, localized, and tailor-made solutions for our partners across the development sector

M&E System Set-up

Comprehensive monitoring and evaluation frameworks designed to track progress, measure impact, and ensure accountability in your programs. We build systems that provide actionable insights for evidence-based decision-making.

Data Management Set-up

Robust data management solutions that ensure data quality, security, and accessibility. From database design to data visualization dashboards, we help you harness the power of your data.

Survey and Assessment

Expert survey design and implementation across various thematic areas. We conduct rigorous assessments that provide reliable data to inform program design and strategic planning.

Evaluation Services

Independent and comprehensive program evaluations that measure effectiveness, efficiency, and impact. Our evaluations provide clear recommendations for program improvement and scaling.

Tailored Training

Customized training programs on data collection tools including ODK, KoboToolbox, and SurveyCTO. We build your team's capacity to collect, manage, and analyze quality data independently.

Consultancy Linkage

Access to our network of experienced freelance consultants and partner organizations. We connect you with the right expertise for your specific needs and project requirements.
