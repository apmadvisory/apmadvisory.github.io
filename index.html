What We Offer

Our Services

We provide innovative, localized, and tailor-made solutions for our partners across the development sector

M&E System Set-up

Comprehensive monitoring and evaluation frameworks designed to track progress, measure impact, and ensure accountability in your programs.

  • Custom M&E framework aligned with program goals
  • Indicator development and measurement tools
  • Data collection and reporting templates
  • Staff training on M&E best practices

Data Management Set-up

Robust data management solutions including database design, data quality protocols, and interactive dashboards that transform raw data into actionable insights.

  • Database design and configuration
  • Data quality assurance protocols
  • Secure data storage systems
  • Interactive visualization dashboards (Tableau, PowerBI)

Survey and Assessment

Expert survey design and implementation including baseline surveys, needs assessments, and KAP studies that provide reliable data to inform program design and strategic planning.

  • Questionnaire design and testing
  • Mobile data collection (ODK, KoboToolbox)
  • Field team training and supervision
  • Data analysis and reporting

Evaluation Services

Independent baseline, midline, endline, and impact evaluations that measure program effectiveness, efficiency, and impact with clear recommendations for improvement.

  • Baseline, midline, and endline evaluations
  • Impact assessments and outcome mapping
  • Stakeholder consultations and analysis
  • Actionable recommendations for scaling

Tailored Training

Customized training programs on data collection tools, M&E methodologies, and data analysis to build your team's capacity for independent, quality data management.

  • ODK, KoboToolbox, and SurveyCTO training
  • M&E fundamentals and best practices
  • Data analysis and visualization skills
  • Hands-on practice with real datasets

Third-Party Monitoring & Evaluation

Independent monitoring and evaluation services providing objective oversight, real-time tracking, and accountability for development programs with unbiased verification.

  • Independent verification of activities and outputs
  • Real-time monitoring and progress tracking
  • Compliance assessment with donor standards
  • Objective reporting and recommendations
