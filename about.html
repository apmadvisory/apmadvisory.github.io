About Us

Who We Are

APM Consultancy is a Myanmar-based consultancy firm specializing in monitoring, evaluation, and data management for civil society organizations and development programs. Founded by experienced M&E professionals, we combine technical expertise with deep local knowledge to deliver practical, evidence-based solutions that maximize program impact and strengthen organizational capacity.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Together with local partners, we empower civil society and development organizations with data-driven insights and innovative solutions that enhance their impact, strengthen accountability, and foster evidence-based decision-making for a more effective and equitable society.

Our Mission

We provide innovative, localized, and tailor-made solutions for our partners. Every organization is unique, and we believe in creating customized approaches that respect local contexts, build on existing strengths, and deliver sustainable results.

Our Approach

Governance & Project Management

Project-Based Teams

We assemble the right team for each project, ensuring expertise matches client needs. Our flexible structure allows us to scale from individual consultants to full survey teams.

Clear Roles & Accountability

Every project has a designated project manager with full operational authority and responsibility, ensuring clear communication and efficient delivery.

Risk Management

We carefully assess legal and reputational risks for all engagements. When projects involve non-registered organizations, politically sensitive work, or human rights issues, comprehensive risk assessments are conducted and require unanimous approval from all members to proceed. This ensures our work maintains the highest standards of safety while protecting both our team and partners.
